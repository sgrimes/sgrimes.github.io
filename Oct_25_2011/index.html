Stephen Grimes

I am working at the Linguistic Data Consortium at the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia.

At the Linguistic Data Consortium, I am involved with the language technology project GALE, Global Autonomous Language Exploitation, a DARPA project. I obtain word alignment judgments for Chinese-English and Arabic-English parallel treebanks, and this data is useful for developing statistical machine translation systems. I am also supporting handwriting recognition projects for Arabic and Chinese languages as part of the MADCAT program.
