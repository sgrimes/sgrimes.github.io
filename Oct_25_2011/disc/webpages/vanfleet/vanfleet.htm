Welcome to Jim Van Fleet's Home Page

I am the Librarian/Information Specialist for Science and Engineering Resources at Bucknell University, Lewisburg, PA. Information Services and Resources includes the Bertrand Library, Computer Services, and the Information Commons, with services at the Reference Desk and the Technology Desk. The ISR Home Page features our Online Catalog, Research Tools, Computing and Technology Resources, and other services.
What Can a Librarian Do For You? My job description and areas of expertise. Other work-related activities, including information on the ISR Disaster Response Team and Bucknell student clubs I advise.
My Star Wars Page. Since military history and science fiction are two of my leisure-time interests, of course my favorite movies are the original Star Wars trilogy. My Star Wars page includes my favorite links, images, and card collecting information.
The Lord of the Rings. My favorite book is Tolkien's Lord of the Rings, and Decipher has created a TCG based on the new movie. Here's some detailed information on the card game.
I've begun to take an "academic interest" in Japanese anime. The kids may like Pokémon, but there is a whole lot more to this art form. Here are my thoughts on building an academic library collection of anime.
Antique Board and Card Games. My wife collects 'em, and the whole family plays. No plug, no buttons, no TV screen. View some neat old games, and find out about the Association of Game and Puzzle Collectors.
