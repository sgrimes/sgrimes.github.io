Welcome to Jim Van Fleet's Home Page

I am the Librarian/Information Specialist for Science and Engineering Resources at Bucknell University, Lewisburg, PA. As we evolve into a new Information Services and Resources organization, the Bertrand Library has developed an Information Commons with services at the Reference/Information Desk and the Technology/Media Help Desk. The ISR Home Page features our Online Catalog, electronic research resources, and other services.
What Can a Librarian Do For You? My job description and areas of expertise. Links to science and engineering information resources on the Internet. Other work-related activities, including information on the Disaster Team and Bucknell student clubs I advise.
My Star Wars Page. Since military history and science fiction are two of my leisure-time interests, of course my favorite movies are the Star Wars trilogy. My Star Wars page includes my favorite links, images, cards, and information on the Star Wars CCG.
I've begun to take an "academic interest" in Japanese anime. The kids may like Pokémon, but there is a whole lot more to this genré. Here are my thoughts on building an academic library collection of anime.
Antique Board and Card Games. My wife collects 'em, and the whole family plays. View some neat old games, find out about the Association of Game and Puzzle Collectors.
