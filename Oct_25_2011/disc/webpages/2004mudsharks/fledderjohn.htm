MATTHEW FLEDDERJOHN

Known Aliases: Maximus, Maxi Pads, Professor Nash

As a bona fide Russell Crowe look-alike, Max personifies all the characteristics of that once mighty empire known as Rome. With the heart and fearlessness of a gladiator in the coliseum, he plays every point as though it was his last, slashing the throat of many with his hardcore fakes. His throws have the precision and deadly accuracy of a skilled swordsman and he is sure to be a weapon on both the MudSharks offense and defense this season. With the strength of the Roman legions, the speed of the swiftest of chariots, and the genius of Julius Ceasar himself, he is the enemy's worst nightmare.
