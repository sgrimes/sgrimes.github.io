STEPHEN TRAN

Known Aliases: Scuba Steve, Catfish

If video games have taught me anything, it's that tiny guys = fast. Scuba steve fits this stereotype perfectly. Primarily a handler or middle, his quickness makes him impossible to guard, and his stamina means he'll just get impossibler to guard as the game goes on. His short stature doesn't keep him from busting long on occassion, though, so you'll have to keep on your toes... OR DIE!
