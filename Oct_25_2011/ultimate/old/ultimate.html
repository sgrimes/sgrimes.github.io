Email lists

These email lists were set up in 2001 as a way for the Bucknell ultimate community to stay in touch. The main purpose is to organize reunion teams for tournaments -- both regional tournaments and Bucknell's Shroomfest. In retrospect, it should have been one list instead of two. The Mudsharks use the list regularly, but the Peace Frog list isn't really in use at all.
